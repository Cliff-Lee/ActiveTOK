## Slide Sequence

1. Activity frame: category, time, big idea, and visual prompt.
2. Purpose and setup: learning focus, materials, and teacher move.
3. Concrete examples: ready-to-use stimulus cards and teacher prompt.

5. Thinking record: observation, inference, evidence, and confidence.

7. Adapt and extend: sample student output, extensions, and run resources.
